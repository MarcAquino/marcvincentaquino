TAKURAN FALLS

TOURIST SPOT IN THE PHILIPPINES

1.Tukuran Falls, located in Sitio Ibuye, San Teodoro, Oriental Mindoro, is a hidden gem. Its name, "Tukuran," comes from the surrounding "tukud-tukod" rocks. While not as famous as other waterfalls, its unique charm lies in its adventurous access—a carabao-cart ride or a 30-minute trek and river wade with a local guide—and its own distinct beauty.
